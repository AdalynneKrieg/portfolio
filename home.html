Home

Hello, my name is Ady Krieg and I am an aspiring 17-year-old artist. I've created this website as an easily accessible way to introduce others
to my work as I seek out new opportunities and friends within the tattoo artistry community!

I was a part of the 2024-2025 AP Art program at Forest Hills Northern. This opportunity has provided me with a lot of one-on-one time with a talented mentor, my teacher Mrs. St Antoine.
Her direction and my own creativity and passion for art have helped immensely with improving my skill and understanding of art.

I am looking for any and all advice, suggestions, connections and mentors, so please feel free to reach out using the Contact page.
