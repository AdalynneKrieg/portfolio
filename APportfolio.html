AP Art Portfolio 2025

Sustained Investigation

trauma bonds and my experiences

For my sustained investigation, I want to explore a specific time in my life that is difficult for me to recall because of the trauma I experienced. To defend itself, my brain has blocked out large portions of my memory from my time spent across the country in an eating disorder clinic. In a desperate attempt to remember and find the good in such a scarring experience, I am going to visually depict as much as I can remember, in as much detail as possible, with help from a close friend I made while there. As I plan and complete this sustained investigation, with every step of the process I will be asking myself what happened, and how did it affect me and the others who also experienced it?
